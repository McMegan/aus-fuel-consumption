Australian State & Territory Fuel Consumption Profile (2019/2020)

Compare the mix of fuels consumed as energy of a region1 to other regions in Australia or to Australia as a whole. Compare fuel consumption in terms of total consumption, as a percentage of regional consumption, per capita and per million dollars of Gross State Product.

1 Region is used to refer to both states and territories of Australia, as well as to Australia as a whole.
